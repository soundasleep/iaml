An \modelLink{Input Form} represents a group of related \modelLink{Visible Things}. An \modelLink{Input Form} cannot accept or lose focus. The \event{onInput} \modelLink{Event Trigger} can never be triggered for an \modelLink{Input Form}. The \modelProperty{Input Form}{name} of the \modelLink{Input Text Field} may be used as a label or heading for the rendered form. The \modelProperty{Input Form}{field value} of the \modelLink{Input Form} has no renderable effect, and may be used to store temporary data.
