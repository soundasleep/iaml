An {@model InputForm} represents a group of related {@model VisibleThings}. An {@model InputForm} cannot accept or lose focus. The {@event onInput} {@model Event} can never be triggered for an {@model InputForm}. The {@model InputForm#name} of the {@model InputTextField} may be used as a label or heading for the rendered form. The {@model InputForm#fieldValue} of the {@model InputForm} has no renderable effect, and may be used to store temporary data.
